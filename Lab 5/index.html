About Matthew Brand

Biography

Hello, my name is Matthew Brand. I am a 3/5 computer science student at Stevens Insitute of Technology. I grew up on Long Island, New York. I am currently in the co-op program at Stevens, and I will be interning at Prudential during the Fall 2018 semester. During the winter and summer breaks I am a manager at an independent movie theater in my hometown.

I have always been passionate about computer science and entrepreneurship. During high school I created two small businesses, one being an iOS app called Twythm and the other a digital coupon service for local businesses called Passly. At Stevens, I co-founded Attila Entrepreneurs, which is the first entreprenurship club at Stevens. I currently serve as it's treasurer.
